About allaRomana

People, and their content, are what gives a social network its value.
We think that user behaviour and personal data have alreaby been abused enough and that it is time for a change. At allaRomana we recognise the value generated by users by sharing our revenues with them.

An algorithm will be analysing user generated content and will assign an Engagement Score to each user of the network. The Engagement Score is calculated in the same way for all the platform users and is the unique parametre used for revenue sharing between users.
